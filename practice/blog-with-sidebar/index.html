Salt Water - Sexy Shirmp

Appearance and Size: Sexy shrimp are very small, typically less than 2 cm (0.8 inches) in length, with females slightly larger than males. They have a translucent brown body adorned with white or blue spots outlined in dark brown, and their abdomen is characteristically curved upwards with the tail fan above the head, giving them their "sexy" appearance. They have short-stalked, protruding eyes and jointed limbs typical of arthropods

Habitat and Distribution: Sexy shrimp are circumtropical, found in shallow coral reefs across the Indo-Pacific, Red Sea, Caribbean, and parts of the Atlantic, including the Canary Islands and Bahamas. They typically live among the tentacles of sea anemones or mushroom corals, forming a commensal relationship where the shrimp gain protection and the host benefits from cleaning of trapped plankton and debris. Occasionally, they can be found free-living in dead corals, rock crevices, or biogenic gravel.
